Understanding Water Quality

pH Level: Water should have a pH between 6.5 and 8.5. Too acidic or too alkaline water can be harmful.

Turbidity: Measured in NTU (Nephelometric Turbidity Units), turbidity should be below 5 to ensure clarity and safety.

TDS (Total Dissolved Solids): Safe drinking water should have TDS below 500 ppm for good taste and health.
